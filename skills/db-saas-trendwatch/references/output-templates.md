## Weekly brief (default)

Title: SaaS DB Trend Brief (YYYY-MM-DD)

1) Top signals (3-5 bullets)
- Signal, company, capability, status, impact

2) What changed since last update
- New architecture patterns
- New incidents or reliability changes
- Pricing or SLAs affecting data tiers

3) Pattern shifts
- Tenancy model shifts
- Migration drivers
- Search/vector adoption

4) Risks and unknowns
- Missing evidence
- Anecdotal claims
- Region/SKU constraints

5) Watch list (next 2-4 weeks)
- Expected releases
- Conferences or talks
- Known migrations or upgrades
