# Source Map (prioritized)

Use `references/watchlist.md` as the default list of companies and people to track. Expand or trim based on user scope.

## Tier 1: Primary signals (highest weight)
- Product docs and release notes (feature status, limitations, regions).
- Pricing/entitlements pages (GA signals, SKU gating, add-on costs).
- Security/compliance docs (enterprise readiness signals).
- Public GitHub repos and release tags (if applicable).

## Tier 2: Company announcements (medium weight)
- Official product and engineering blogs.
- Conference keynotes and launch pages.
- Customer case studies with technical detail.

## Tier 3: Market and research (context signals)
- Analyst reports and market sizing: IDC, Gartner.
- Peer-reviewed research: ACM, VLDB, SIGMOD.
- Practitioner essays: Medium and engineering blogs.

## Tier 4: Media and social (watch list)
- Business media for M&A, funding, hiring: Bloomberg.
- Cloud/data leader posts (CTO/VP, principal engineers).
- Community forums and issue trackers (early feedback).

## Company-specific sources to include
- Databricks: docs, release notes, blog, Data + AI Summit pages.
- Snowflake: docs, release notes, blog, Snowflake Summit pages.
- Microsoft: Fabric/Azure docs, release notes, Fabric blog, Ignite/Build.
- Google: Cloud/BigQuery docs and release notes, Google Cloud Next.
- Airbnb: Engineering blog and technical talks.
- Pinterest: Engineering blog and technical talks.

## Suggested scan cadence
- Weekly: Tier 1 + Tier 2 for the watchlist.
- Monthly: Tier 3 reports and research venues.
- Quarterly: Conference recap sweep (keynotes, new products, pricing changes).
